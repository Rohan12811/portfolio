Marri Timber Kitchen

Visualisation for Kitchen Renovation

Modelled from the ground up using provided AutoCAD drawings, this project was created to communicate the planned renovation of a private kitchen and the palette options available. Custom timber material nodes were developed to accurately emulate West Australian timbers, ensuring the visualisation reflected both the design intent and the material authenticity.

Product

Niche Box Trio

By Scarab Studio Wood

A polished 3D visualisation of a compound timber product designed by Scarab Studio Wood. This render set highlights the sculpted beauty of West Australian sheoak and jarrah while also revealing the interlocking mechanics. The geometry was developed from AutoCAD handover drawings and rebuilt in Blender. The final delivery included product variation stills, a looping exploded view, and a detailed product assembly animation. I find this type of work especially satisfying and would welcome the opportunity to take on more projects like it.

Product & Packaging

Western Honey

Product and Packaging Visualisation

A set of product and packaging renders created for Western Honey as part of their brand presentation. The focus was on keeping the visuals simple and natural, with clean geometry, warm lighting, and careful material choices to reflect the product’s identity. These images were produced to support both marketing and client presentations, intended to showcase the project before production.
